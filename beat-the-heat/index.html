Questions

What people ask before booking.

How much can curtains and blinds actually reduce indoor temperature?
With the right thermal fabric and blackout lining, indoor temperatures can drop 6–8°C versus untreated glass. Solar roller blinds with low openness factor block the UV without darkening the room — that alone gets you 3–4°C in most apartments.
Which is better for Dubai heat — curtains or blinds?
For pure heat performance, thermal-lined curtains drop the most degrees because they trap an insulating air layer. Solar blinds keep the view. We usually recommend layering both for south- and west-facing rooms.
Do you have fabrics that block UV without making the room dark?
Yes — solar roller blind fabrics at 3% and 5% openness block 95–97% of UV while still letting daylight through. You keep the view, you lose the heat.
How long does the whole process take?
Free home visit usually happens within 48 hours. Manufacturing takes 3–5 working days. Installation is one half-day visit. Total: typically under one week.
Which areas of Dubai do you serve?
All of Dubai — from the Marina to Arabian Ranches to Jumeirah to Silicon Oasis. Same one-week turnaround wherever you are.
