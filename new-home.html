Common Questions

Good questions. Honest answers.

What does "free installation" include?

+
Professional installation of all curtains and blinds — including tracks, brackets, hardware, and any drilling. We also remove and dispose of any old fittings completely free. Zero labour costs.

How quickly can you fit out my new home?

+
Most move-in packages are completed within one week of your home visit. For urgent move-ins, we often turn around within 48–72 hours. We know you want to settle in fast.

Can I mix curtains and blinds in one package?

+
Absolutely — that's what most customers do. Blackout curtains in bedrooms, sheers in the living room, roller blinds in the kitchen and bathrooms. We'll advise room by room during your home visit.

Do I need to measure my windows first?

+
Not at all — that's exactly what the home visit is for. Our team brings professional measuring equipment and handles everything on-site. All products are custom-cut to your exact dimensions.

I'm a tenant — is it worth it?

+
Most landlords in Dubai don't provide curtains or blinds, and bare windows make any home feel unfinished. Our fittings don't cause wall damage, and good window treatments make your space dramatically more comfortable from day one.

Is the home visit really free?

+
Completely free, completely no-obligation. We visit, measure every window, advise on the best setup per room, and give you a full package quote. You decide in your own time.
