Questions

From clients who just moved in.

How quickly can you get everything installed after handover?
Typically 7–10 days from your home visit. We measure on day 1, manufacture days 2–6, install days 7–10. For larger villas we may split installation across two days.
What's actually included in each package?
Every window in the home: measurement, fabric, tracks, motorisation (where stated), professional installation, and removal of any existing fittings. No hidden line items.
Can we mix and match — curtains in some rooms, blinds in others?
Absolutely — that's what most clients do. Roller blinds for the kitchen, sheer + blackout in the bedrooms, full curtains in the living. Your package adapts to your home.
Do you work in Abu Dhabi and Sharjah?
Yes — same packages, same prices, same one-week turnaround. Free home visit in all three emirates.
Can we pay in instalments?
Yes. 50% on order to start manufacturing, 50% on installation day. We also offer 3-month split with selected UAE banks for The Estate and Signature packages.
