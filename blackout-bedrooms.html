Questions

Before you book.

What's the difference between "blackout" and "room darkening"?
Room-darkening fabric blocks roughly 80–95% of light. True blackout blocks 99–100% and has a coated lining. Plus the install matters — even blackout fabric leaks if the curtain is cut narrow or hung short.
Do you have curtains that block light from the sides too?
Yes. We measure with 8–10cm side returns and over-length drop so curtains overlap the wall on both sides and pool at the floor. That's how you eliminate the side and bottom light strips.
Can I get blackout curtains for a kid's room with a fun fabric?
Absolutely. We add a separate blackout lining behind your chosen face fabric. The room looks bright and cheerful. The room sleeps pitch black. Many parents pick velvet stars or palm patterns for the face.
How long does installation take?
Half a day for a typical bedroom. Most clients book a Saturday morning slot and have everything hanging by lunch.
Are motorised curtains worth it?
For one or two bedrooms with a daily routine — yes. Schedule a gentle open at sunrise and a hard close at bedtime. Particularly worth it for shift workers and parents of young kids. Probably overkill for guest rooms.
