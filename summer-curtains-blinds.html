Common Questions

Good questions. Honest answers.

What does "free installation" include exactly?

+
Professional installation of all curtains and blinds at no extra charge — including tracks, brackets, hardware, and any drilling. We also remove and dispose of your old fittings completely free. Zero labour costs.

Can I get curtains and blinds together?

+
Absolutely — that's what most customers do. Blackout curtains in bedrooms, sheers in the living room, roller blinds in the kitchen and bathrooms. We advise room by room during your free home visit. One visit, one quote, one installation day.

How quickly can you fit out my home?

+
Most orders are completed within 3–7 days of your home visit. Full move-in packages are typically done within one week. For urgent cases, we can turn around in 48–72 hours.

Are the fabrics built for Dubai's extreme heat?

+
Yes — we specifically select fabrics tested for UAE conditions. All materials are rated for UV resistance, heat blocking, and colour stability in extreme sunlight. They won't fade or degrade in 2–3 years like cheap alternatives.

What if I only want to do one or two rooms?

+
Absolutely fine. Many customers start with the bedroom — that's where heat and light impact sleep the most — and come back for the rest. No minimum order and free installation applies regardless.

Is the home visit really free with no obligation?

+
Completely free, completely no-obligation. We come, measure every window, advise on the best setup per room, and quote. You decide in your own time. No pressure.
